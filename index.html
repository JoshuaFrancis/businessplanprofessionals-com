How it works

A structured intake, a matched writer, and a delivered plan. No surprises.

01

Discovery and Intake

You complete a structured intake form covering your business, your goal, and your target audience. Takes 45 minutes. No call required before the form is submitted.

02

Writer Matching

Your file is reviewed and assigned to the writer whose credentials and experience match your plan type and lender. You are told who is working on your file before writing begins.

03

Writing and Review

Narrative and financial model are written in parallel, then reviewed by a second team member before delivery. The internal review catches inconsistencies before the document reaches you.

04

Delivery and Revision

Final PDF and editable source files delivered to your inbox. One revision round included. Revisions returned within 12 hours of receiving your feedback. Ready to submit the same week.
